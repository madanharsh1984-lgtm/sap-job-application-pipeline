## 🚀 What Is JobAccelerator AI?

JobAccelerator AI is a SaaS platform that helps job seekers automate their entire job application pipeline. It connects to major job boards, tailors your resume, and submits applications on your behalf — saving you hours of manual effort every day.

### Key Features

- **Automated Job Discovery** — Finds relevant openings from LinkedIn, Indeed, Naukri, and more
- **AI Resume Optimization** — Tailors your resume to each job description
- **One-Click Apply** — Submits applications automatically across platforms
- **Smart Dashboard** — Track all your applications, responses, and analytics in one place
- **Integration Hub** — Connect LinkedIn, email, and other services seamlessly
- **Payment & Subscription Management** — Simple plans with transparent pricing
