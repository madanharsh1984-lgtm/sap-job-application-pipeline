# Getting Started with JobAccelerator AI

Welcome! This guide will help you understand what JobAccelerator AI does and walk you through your first successful automated job application.

---

## What Is JobAccelerator AI?

JobAccelerator AI is a SaaS platform that **automates your entire job search pipeline**. Instead of spending hours each day browsing job boards, customizing resumes, and clicking "Apply," JobAccelerator AI does it all for you.

### How It Works

1. **You set your preferences** — job titles, locations, experience level, and target companies
2. **AI finds matching jobs** — scans LinkedIn, Indeed, Naukri, and other platforms daily
3. **AI tailors your resume** — customizes your resume for each job description
4. **AI applies for you** — submits applications automatically on supported platforms
5. **You track results** — monitor applications, responses, and interview invitations from your dashboard

## Quick Start — 5 Steps to Your First Application

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to [app.jobaccelerator.ai](https://app.jobaccelerator.ai)
2. Click the **Sign Up** button on the homepage
3. Enter your name, email address, and create a password
4. Click **Create Account**
5. Check your email for a verification link and click it

### Step 2: Upload Your Resume

1. After logging in, you will be taken to the **Onboarding Wizard**
2. Click **Upload Resume** on the first onboarding screen
3. Select your resume file (PDF or DOCX, max 10 MB)
4. Wait for the AI to parse and analyze your resume
5. Review the extracted skills and experience, then click **Confirm**

> **Pro Tip:** Upload your most recent and comprehensive resume. The AI uses this as the foundation for all tailored applications.

---

### Step 3: Set Your Job Preferences

1. On the next onboarding screen, set your **Job Preferences**:
   - **Job Titles** — e.g., "Software Engineer," "Product Manager"
   - **Locations** — e.g., "Remote," "New York, NY," "Bangalore"
   - **Experience Level** — Entry, Mid, Senior, Lead, Executive
   - **Salary Range** (optional) — Set minimum expected compensation
   - **Industries** (optional) — e.g., "Technology," "Finance"
2. Click **Save Preferences**

### Step 4: Connect Your Accounts

1. On the **Integrations** screen, connect at least one job platform:
   - Click **Connect LinkedIn** and log in with your LinkedIn credentials
   - (Optional) Click **Connect Naukri** or **Connect Indeed**
2. Connect your email account for application tracking:
   - Click **Connect Gmail** or **Connect Outlook**
3. Click **Finish Setup**

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Your LinkedIn and job board credentials are encrypted and stored securely. JobAccelerator AI never shares your login information with third parties.

---

### Step 5: Activate Automation & Apply

1. From the **Dashboard**, click the **Start Automation** button
2. Review the automation summary:
   - Number of matching jobs found
   - Platforms that will be used
   - Resume version that will be submitted
3. Click **Confirm & Start**
4. Your first batch of applications will begin processing
