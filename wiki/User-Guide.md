## A. Account Creation

### Creating a New Account

1. Navigate to [app.jobaccelerator.ai](https://app.jobaccelerator.ai)
2. Click the **Sign Up** button in the top-right corner
3. Fill in the registration form:
   - **Full Name** — Your first and last name
   - **Email Address** — Use a valid email you check regularly
   - **Password** — Minimum 8 characters, including one uppercase letter and one number
4. Read and accept the **Terms of Service** and **Privacy Policy**
5. Click **Create Account**
6. Open your email inbox and click the **Verify Email** link

## C. Onboarding Flow

After your first login, you'll be guided through a 3-step onboarding wizard.

### Step 1: Resume Upload

1. On the **Upload Resume** screen, click **Choose File** or drag your resume into the upload area
2. Supported formats: **PDF**, **DOCX** (max 10 MB)
3. Wait for the AI to parse your resume (typically 10–30 seconds)
4. Review the extracted information:
   - Contact details
   - Work experience
   - Skills and technologies
   - Education and certifications
5. Edit any incorrect information by clicking the **Edit** icon next to each section
6. Click **Confirm & Continue**

> **Pro Tip:** If the parser misses some details, you can manually edit them. The more accurate your profile, the better the AI matches you with jobs.

### Step 2: Integration Setup

1. On the **Connect Accounts** screen, you'll see available integrations:
   - **LinkedIn** — Required for LinkedIn Easy Apply
   - **Indeed** — Optional
   - **Naukri** — Optional
   - **Gmail / Outlook** — For email-based applications and tracking
2. Click **Connect** next to each platform you want to use
3. You will be redirected to the platform's login page — enter your credentials
4. After successful authentication, you'll see a **Connected** badge
5. Connect at least **one job platform** and **one email account**
6. Click **Continue**

### Step 3: Job Preferences

1. On the **Preferences** screen, configure:
   - **Job Titles** — Add 1–5 target job titles (e.g., "SAP Consultant," "Program Manager")
   - **Locations** — Select preferred locations or choose "Remote"
   - **Experience Level** — Select your current level
   - **Minimum Salary** (optional) — Set a floor for job matches
   - **Industries** (optional) — Filter by industry
   - **Excluded Companies** (optional) — Companies you don't want to apply to
2. Click **Save & Finish**

## E. Viewing Jobs

### Browsing Job Matches

1. Click **Jobs** in the left sidebar menu
2. The jobs page shows all discovered job matches, sorted by relevance
3. Each job card displays:
   - Job title and company name
   - Location (or "Remote")
   - Salary range (if available)
   - Match score (percentage fit based on your resume)
   - Platform source (LinkedIn, Indeed, etc.)
   - Posted date

[Screenshot: Jobs list page with multiple job cards]
> **Instruction:** Capture the jobs page showing 3–5 job cards in a list or grid view. Each card should display title, company, location, match score, and source platform.

### Viewing Job Details

1. Click on any job card to open the **Job Details** page
2. Review:
   - Full job description
   - Required qualifications
   - Your resume match analysis (skills matched vs. missing)
   - Application status (Not Applied / Applied / Response Received)
3. To apply manually, click **Apply Now**
4. To skip this job, click **Dismiss**

[Screenshot: Job details page with match analysis]
> **Instruction:** Capture a job detail view showing the full description, a skill match breakdown (e.g., "8/10 skills matched"), and the "Apply Now" and "Dismiss" buttons.

### Filtering and Sorting Jobs

1. Use the **Filter Bar** at the top of the jobs page:
   - **Platform** — LinkedIn, Indeed, Naukri, All
   - **Location** — Remote, On-site, Hybrid
   - **Match Score** — Minimum match percentage (e.g., 70%+)
   - **Date Posted** — Last 24 hours, Last 7 days, Last 30 days
2. Use the **Sort** dropdown to order by:
   - Relevance (default)
   - Date Posted (newest first)
   - Match Score (highest first)
   - Salary (highest first)

## F. Resume Update

### Updating Your Resume

1. Click **Profile** in the left sidebar menu
2. Click the **Resume** tab
3. You can either:
   - **Upload a new resume** — Click **Upload New** and select a file
   - **Edit your current profile** — Click **Edit** on any section (Experience, Skills, Education)
4. After making changes, click **Save Changes**
5. The AI will re-analyze your resume and update your match scores

[Screenshot: Profile page — Resume tab]
> **Instruction:** Capture the profile page with the Resume tab selected, showing the current resume summary with "Upload New" and "Edit" buttons visible.

[Screenshot: Editing skills section]
> **Instruction:** Capture the skills editing interface showing current skills as tags/chips with the ability to add or remove skills.

> **Pro Tip:** Update your resume whenever you gain new skills, complete a project, or earn a certification. This keeps your match scores accurate.

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Uploading a new resume replaces the existing one. If you want to keep multiple versions, download the current version first.

### Resume Versions

1. Go to **Profile** → **Resume** → **Version History**
2. View all previously uploaded resumes with timestamps
3. Click **Restore** to revert to a previous version
4. Click **Download** to save a copy to your computer

## I. Daily Usage Flow

Here's what a typical day looks like with JobAccelerator AI:

### Morning (Automated)

1. **9:00 AM** — JobAccelerator AI scans all connected platforms for new job postings
2. **9:15 AM** — AI matches jobs against your preferences and assigns match scores
3. **9:30 AM** — AI begins submitting applications for high-match jobs
4. **10:00 AM** — You receive a **Daily Summary** email with:
   - Number of new matches found
   - Number of applications submitted
   - Any issues requiring your attention

[Screenshot: Daily summary email]
> **Instruction:** Capture a sample daily summary email showing job match count, applications submitted count, and a "View Dashboard" button.

### During the Day

5. **Check Dashboard** — Review new matches and application statuses
6. **Review responses** — Check for interview invitations or follow-up requests
7. **Dismiss irrelevant jobs** — Help the AI learn your preferences by dismissing poor matches

### Evening (Optional)

8. **Adjust preferences** — Add or remove job titles, update locations
9. **Update resume** — Add new skills or recent project experience
10. **Review analytics** — Check your response rate and top-performing job titles

[Screenshot: Dashboard — end of day view with activity feed]

## Best Practices

1. **Keep your resume current** — Update it after every new project or certification
2. **Use specific job titles** — "SAP FICO Consultant" is better than "Consultant"
3. **Start with fewer preferences** — Begin focused, then broaden if needed
4. **Review daily summaries** — Act on interview invitations quickly
5. **Re-authenticate periodically** — Some platforms require periodic re-login
